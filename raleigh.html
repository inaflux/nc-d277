Raleigh, North Carolina

Known as the "City of Oaks" for its lush tree-lined streets, Raleigh offers a unique blend of history, culture, and technology. Whether you're a visitor or a resident, Raleigh has something special to offer everyone.

5 Facts About Raleigh

  1. City's Population: As of 2023, the estimated population of Raleigh is approximately 476,587​
  2. Year Incorporated: Raleigh was incorporated in the year 1792​
  3. Region: Raleigh is located in the Piedmont region of North Carolina
  4. City Classification: Raleigh is classified as an urban city​
  5. Average Income Level: The median household income in Raleigh is around $65,695, which is higher than the North Carolina state average of $54,602​ ​
*These are important facts about Raleigh, North Carolina
