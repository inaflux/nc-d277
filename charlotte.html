Charlotte, North Carolina

Charlotte, North Carolina, affectionately known as the "Queen City," is a vibrant and rapidly growing metropolis that beautifully blends its rich history with modern innovation.

5 Facts About Charlotte

  1. Population: As of 2023, Charlotte's population is estimated to be approximately 897,720​
  2. Year Incorporated: Charlotte was incorporated in 1768​
  3. Region: Charlotte is located in the Piedmont​ region of North Carolina​
  4. Classification: Charlotte is classified as an urban​ city​
  5. Average Income Level: The average income of a Charlotte resident is$43,080 per year, and the median household income is $68,367 per year​
