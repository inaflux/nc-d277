Asheville, North Carolina

Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Asheville is a vibrant city known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and eclectic arts scene.

5 Facts About Asheville

  1. Population: As of 2023, the population of Asheville is estimated to be 95,056​
  2. Year Incorporated: Asheville was incorporated in the year 1797​
  3. Region: Asheville is located in Western North Carolina
  4. City Classification: Asheville is classified as an urban city​
  5. Average Income Level: The median household income in Asheville is around $53,621, which is slightly below than the North Carolina state average of $54,602​ ​
